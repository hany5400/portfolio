Project 1: Candy Line Sales

These dashboards present candy line sales performance, highlighting key metrics such as total profit, total sales value, number of regions, total orders, number of customers, top state and city, and division performance. The two designs demonstrate different layout and visualization approaches, both aimed at delivering clear, actionable insights for decision-making.

Project 2: Sales Performance

This dashboard offers a comprehensive view of sales performance, showing units sold, revenue before and after tax, and overall profit, while highlighting top states and cities. It combines geographic insights, package-type comparisons, and time-based trends to give decision-makers a clear understanding of sales patterns, growth opportunities, and key profit drivers.

Project 3: HR Analysis

This HR dashboard presents insights on 1,326 employees with a balanced gender split. It highlights top education fields, attrition by role (highest in Machine Learning and Engineering Managers), and performance ratings, where most meet or exceed expectations. The average manager rating is 10.4, and most employees are based in California. Salaries total 113 million, led by Senior Software Engineers, while departmental distribution shows Technology as the largest group, followed by Sales and HR.
